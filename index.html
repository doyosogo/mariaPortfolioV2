About

A bit more about me

Coffee for me is as much about people as it is about flavour. I love the rhythm of busy café shifts—dialing in, pouring latte art, and keeping the flow smooth—but what I enjoy most is connecting with customers. I’ve been told my energy and charisma make people feel comfortable, whether it’s a quick chat while serving or remembering a regular’s favourite order. I take pride in creating a warm atmosphere, staying calm under pressure, and making every cup feel personal.

  • Espresso: dial-in, puck prep, shot evaluation
  • Milk: microfoam, latte art (rosettas, tulips, swans)
  • Ops: opening, cleaning, food safety

Equipment touched

  • Espresso machines: Synesso
  • Grinders: Mazzer
  • Scales: Acaia
